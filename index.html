The Influencer Vetting Process

We follow a meticulous approach to ensure that only top-tier influencers are part of our network. This includes:

  • Verification of Authenticity: Ensuring the influencer’s follower base is genuine and engaged, not composed of bots or fake accounts.
  • Content Quality Review: Assessing the quality, relevance, and consistency of the influencer’s content to ensure alignment with brand values.
  • Audience Demographic Analysis: Assessing the influencer’s audience to ensure consistency with the brand’s target audience and values.
  • Engagement Rate Monitoring: Tracking metrics such as likes, comments, and shares to gauge the influencer’s ability to drive meaningful interactions.
